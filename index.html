Comprehensive Corporate Legal Services

From entity formation to complex cross-border disputes, our team provides end-to-end legal solutions engineered around your commercial objectives.

Company Formation & Incorporation

Structuring mainland, free zone, and offshore entities with full guidance on ownership frameworks, licensing, and regulatory compliance under UAE Commercial Companies Law.

Mergers & Acquisitions

Full-service M&A advisory including due diligence, deal structuring, regulatory approvals, and post-merger integration across UAE and cross-border transactions.

Corporate Restructuring & Insolvency

Strategic restructuring, bankruptcy protection, and creditor negotiation under UAE Insolvency Law, preserving enterprise value during periods of financial distress.

Commercial Litigation & Arbitration

Representation before UAE courts and arbitral tribunals with expertise in shareholder disputes, breach of contract, and enforcement of foreign judgments and awards.

Banking & Finance Law

Regulatory compliance, loan documentation, security enforcement, and fintech advisory aligned with UAE Central Bank regulations and cross-border financing frameworks.

Employment & Labour Law

Workforce structuring, executive employment contracts, terminations, and dispute resolution under UAE Labour Law, safeguarding employer interests while maintaining full compliance.
